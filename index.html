About SB MESCE

Our IEEE student branch is your number one station for social and technical development! Our motto is to implant and blossom new technical and innovative ideas in the minds of the future engineers, creating a better world. IEEE SB MESCE is a platform to build up a future you want. This branch was officially formed in 2004 with a motive to help the students of MESCE with their creative ideas. Kicking off with a vibrant start, IEEE SB MESCE conquered places with their ideas and team spirit. In 2009, IEEE SB MESCE won the ‘Best Management Supports’ in the IEEE Kerala Section Awards. We also had 2 teams in the Top 20 of IEEE All India Student Awareness Contest. In 2011, IEEE SB MESCE hosted the LINK Camp. Currently we are progressing with 107 innovative minds, an excellent team of volunteering members that are passionate about what they do.

IEEE RAS SBC MESCE conducted a treasure hunt in the name TREZO-Rise and shine it is huntin’ time, on 5 th of July 2020 through instagram.
The treasure hunt witnessed an active participation of 56 students who competed enthusiastically till the end. The whole competition was set up through instagram where the students had to attempt a variety of questions to grab the jackpot.
The event started at 12:00 pm in the noon and it was a 12 hour treasure hunt which came to an end at 12:00am in the night. There was a cash prize of 400/- for the first to hunt down the treasure.
There was 6 questions in total. The first question was provided on the instagram story of IEEE SB MESCE and participants replying to it with the right answer will be provided with a code which when combined with the answer will provide the participant with the next page and so on. That particular page will hold the next question and an image. At the end there will be 4 images and when they are put together they will get the final image which gives the last question of the hunt. The event was well organized. The participants were very active and cooperative throughout the hunt which helped the event to be a victorious one.

but have you ever wondered what AR and VR actually are?

IEEE RAS SBC MESCE in association with IEEE Kerala Young Professionals conducted a webinar on “Augmented Reality & Virtual Reality” on 29 th of June 2020 , which holds a greater importance in todays technology. The webinar aimed to give a basic idea to the participants on AR & VR, and how it is used.
We were honoured to have Mr. Shyam Pradeep Alil to handle the session. He is the Co-founder and Chief Operations Officer of Infusory Future Tech Labs Pvt Ltd. The event witnessed an active participation of 60+ participants from various colleges.
The webinar was so informative since he included the video clippings of the latest technologies using AR and VR which included the use of AR in sports, VR in medical field etc. He started off the session by giving a brief explanation on AR and VR. He also explained different types of VR devices like Google Cardboard VR, VR box, HTC vive, Oculus Rift, oculus Go etc. He also explained about Unity which is the most widely used VR development platform.
